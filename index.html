Dr. Dr. Niels Skovgaard-Olsen

My work focuses on topics in the intersection of philosophy, psychology, and linguistics (or more broadly: cognitive science).
Some of it is purely theoretical and other is empirical and the focus is on reasoning and language.
My research topics include semantics, pragmatics, rationality, individual variation, and norms.
In addition, I am interested in methodological issues arising from empirical rationality research and computational modelling.
